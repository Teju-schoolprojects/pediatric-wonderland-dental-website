Parent FAQs

Quick answers to typical dental questions parents ask us.

The American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry recommends that a child should visit the dentist by their first birthday or within 6 months of their first tooth erupting. Early visits help children build comfort in the dental space and let us track development from the start.

Our team is specially trained in pediatric behavior guidance. We use "Tell-Show-Do" techniques, speak in comforting child-friendly analogies, offer sensory items like weighted blankets, and provide positive distraction screens. We work at your child's pace and never force treatment.

We are in-network with most major PPO dental insurances. We also offer affordable self-pay pricing and flexible monthly installment plans to ensure every child gets the magical care they deserve. Contact us for a full insurance lookup!
